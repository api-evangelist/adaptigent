What Is Modernization Without Migration and Why Is It Becoming an Enterprise Operating Model? Modernization without migration is the practice of improving enterprise systems through integration, governance, and orchestration rather than replacing core infrastructure. Throughout this blog series, we have examined how organizations can modernize continuously without destabilizing the systems that support revenue, compliance, and daily operations.
